Call for Participation

Digital Research Start-Up Partnerships for Graduate Students, Summer 2017, UCLA Library

Submission deadline: Applications received by May 26 will be notified by June 1. Applications will be accepted through June 15 on a rolling basis if space is available. Please submit your application here and include a project description of no more than 3 pages along with your CV.

The project description should include:

  • Overall research question and brief explanation of its relevance
  • Scholarly goals and objectives for the summer project
  • Materials/sources that will form the basis of the project
  • If digitization is required, describe the source materials, desired output format, digitization process
  • Project deliverables such as a conference paper, journal article, website, etc. (Note: complete dissertations should not be submitted as deliverables)
  • Digital scholarship tools and methods that you expect to use
  • A brief evaluation of your relevant technical skills, identifying areas in which you would benefit from consultation and partnerships with project specialists
  • A realistic schedule/timeline for your project
  • A budget listing the projectsâ€™ funding needs and other sources of support (if available)
